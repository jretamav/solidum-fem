# Code of Conduct

Solidum FEM adopts the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
All participants in the project — maintainers, contributors, and users —
are expected to read it and follow it.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project author at **jretamav@comunidad.unam.mx**.
Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly; all
reporters' privacy will be respected.
